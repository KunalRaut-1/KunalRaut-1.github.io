Six things, done end to end

K
Kunal Raut @DementorHere·services···

What I actually run for a brand, end to end:

1. Brand platform & voice — define the positioning, tone, and rules so every post sounds unmistakably like the brand.

2. Content strategy — map goals to a posting cadence and narrative arc that compounds over weeks, not days.

3. Content buckets — repeatable bucket systems that keep the feed varied, on-message, and easy to scale.

4. Reactive engagement — same-day newsjacking and replies that put the brand inside the conversation in real time.

5. Targeted audience — find and grow the right followers (founders, traders, hiring managers), not vanity numbers.

6. Memetic content — original images & video built to travel. The format that earns the algorithm’s reach.
